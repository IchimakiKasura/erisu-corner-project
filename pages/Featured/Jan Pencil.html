As I’ve introduced, Jan is one of the founding officers of Erisu’s Corner. Why? Jan has been my friend in EARIST since 2018 and I’m always amazed by his talents ever since. He’s a master of the pencil (maybe that’s why it’s his pen name lol), he really does art with passion and dedication. And that’s why he is deemed worthy of handling office as one of our Creative Designs Department artists.

But before Jan entered EARIST, he actually studied Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (IT) in Informatics College but apparently, art is his calling so he stopped taking IT and started focusing on what he really is — an Artist.

“My father introduced me to a college that has the same name as my high school – E. Rodriguez Jr. High School so then, I enrolled in EARIST. I was happy to have great professors that I can talk to. And that’s how I started doing more of what I love!”






“Special Delivery, Revealing the Package” | Oil on Canvas | Oct. 20, 2018 | Event: Pilipinas Shell Awards 51st National Student Art Competition – Nov. 13, 2018



Jan is a student of EARIST’s College of Architecture and Fine Arts, major in Painting (BFA-PTG). As his major states, he mostly establishes his skills in painting and he also does photography.

He also does commissions and in fact, had already sold numerous paintings and sketches. He can also do live commissions, as he did during one of his participations in one of GTMACCON’s events. As of now, he does digital art commissions and have done so with anime and game merchandise.






GTMACCON Art Activity – Dec. 16, 2018 | Commissioned Art – Color Pencils on Paper | Photos Courtesy of Kit Perez, GTMACCON

The most important fact is that, I trusted Jan in creating our mascot, Erisu! Know more about Erisu’s creation in another blog!
